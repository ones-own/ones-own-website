Latest work

We recently launched a new website for Playpress, who make eco-friendly playsets for well-known brands like The Gruffalo. Their old website, built using Squarespace, was painfully slow; taking 23 seconds to load. As 40% of customers will, according to research by Google, leave a website if it takes more than 3 seconds to load, we created a custom theme for Shopify that focused on performance, usability and accessibility using the Kindly Design System.

We started by building a quick-loading and easy to use User Experience (UX) prototype for Playpress's three sales channels: selling playsets to consumers, brands commissioning bespoke playsets and shops wanting to stock playsets. We then work with Matt and Melvin from Playress to layer the look and feel of the Playpress brand onto the UX prototype while ensuring that the site remained fast and accessible.
